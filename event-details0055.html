Notification for Renewal of Fellowship-FCPS (Membership of CPS, Mumbai)
Start Date: 01/03/2019
End Date: 01/09/2019
Notice is given to all the Fellows of CPS (by examination) who have been conferred their fellowship on or before 31st July 2009, for renewal of their membership on the basis of fellowship by examination. 
In terms thereof as per Section XVI (4), of the Bye-Laws of CPS it is mandatory for every fellow to renew his/her membership every 10 years from his date and year of convocation as a fellow of CPS, by paying the fees as decided by the CPS Council from time to time otherwise his/her name shall be removed from the electoral roll.
In view of the above all the Fellows of CPS(FCPS) who have completed 10 years from the date and year of convocation (before 31st July 2009) are hereby directed to renew the membership of CPS as fellow by paying fees of Rs. 1000/- (Rs. One Thousand Only) on or before 1st September, 2019 as decided by CPS Council. It is hereby made clear that, if anyone fails to renew his/her membership, his/her name shall be removed from electoral roll without giving any further notice.  The renewal form is made available on official website of College of Physicians and Surgeons of Mumbai www.cpsmumbai.org and/ or in the office of CPS, Mumbai during office hours. 
           
Registrar
College of Physicians and Surgeons of Mumbai
 
Click Here to download the Renewal Form
 
Please submit duly filled and signed Renewal form and fees (by cash / DD in favour of 'College of Physicians and Surgeons of Mumbai') with Self-attested photocopy of Convocation Certificate (FCPS) and State Medical Council Registration Certificate ID Proof (Aadhar Card).
