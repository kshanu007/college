CPS-PG admissions 2020 for the State of Odisha shall start from 07-09-2020
05/09/2020
NEET-PG-2020 eligible candidates may apply online at https://online.cpsmumbai.org 
Last Date for submission of online application and payment of online application fees shall be 21-09-2020 till 03.00 p.m.
 
For more details, read carefully ‘CPS-PG admissions 2020 - Guidelines for the State of Odisha’ (Please Download PDF file below)
 
Candidates who are unable to pay Rs. 15,000/- application fees online may send Demand Draft (DD) of Rs. 15,000/- for application fees.
DD should reach CPS office, Mumbai on or before 21-09-2020 till 03:00 p.m.
DD should be in favour of ‘’College of Physicians and Surgeons of Mumbai’’ payable at Mumbai.
 
For any query feel free to mail us on cpsonline@cpsmumbai.org.
